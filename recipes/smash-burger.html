Smash Burger

This is an amazing Smash Burger recipe that will make all your friends and family love you.

Ingredients

  • Brioche Buns
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Burger Sauce

Steps

  1. Butter your Brioche Buns and Put them on a Grill or Pan
  2. Prepare your Ground Beef into small balls
  3. Cover your balls with parchment paper and Use your spatula to smash the balls
  4. Season with Salt and Pepper
  5. Spread your sauce on the buns
  6. Assemble and Enjoy !
