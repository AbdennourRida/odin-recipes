Spaghetti

This is an amazing Spaghetti recipe that will make all your friends and family love you.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noods
  • Grandma's Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Pesto
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil your noods in HOT WATER
  2. Cook Garlic and Tomato Sauce with Olive Oil and Add Salt and Pepper
  3. Mix the spaghetti and add some of its water
  4. Add som Pesto on top and Enjoy!
