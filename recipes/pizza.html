Pizza

This is an amazing Margarita Pizza recipe that will make all your friends and family love you.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella Balls
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive oil and Salt

Steps

  1. Kneed your dough to a thin base
  2. Add some of your italian grandma's tomato
  3. Add your sliced San Marzano Tomatoes on top
  4. Add your sliced Fresh Mozzarella Balls
  5. Add Basil, some Olive Oil and a pinch of salt
  6. Cook at 200Celsius for 10-15min
  7. Take it out and Add more Olive Oil
  8. Enjoy your creation !
